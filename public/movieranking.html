The Best Movies According to Austin

My top 3 movies of all time.

A Clockwork Orange

A Clockwork Orange follows the leader of a small gang through a dystopian future. It's delightfully disturbing and jarring.

Indiana Jones and the Lost Ark

A scrappy archeaologist goes on an adventure through the desert to find the Ark of the Covenant before the Nazi's do.

War Dogs

Two young men enter the exciting industry of arms dealing and get up to some silly hijinks.
